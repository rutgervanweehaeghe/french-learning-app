The Passé Composé in French

The Passé Composé is a past tense in French used to describe completed actions.

Formation of the Passé Composé

The Passé Composé is formed using the present tense of an auxiliary verb (être or avoir) followed by the past participle of the main verb.

Regular Verbs in Passé Composé

Regular verbs follow these patterns to form the past participle:

  • -ER verbs: Drop **-er** and add **-é** (e.g., parler → **parlé**)
    (to speak → spoken)
  • -IR verbs: Drop **-ir** and add **-i** (e.g., finir → **fini**)
    (to finish → finished)
  • -RE verbs: Drop **-re** and add **-u** (e.g., vendre → **vendu**)
    (to sell → sold)

1. Passé Composé with "Avoir" (Most Verbs)

Most verbs in French use avoir as the auxiliary.

Subject Parler (to speak) Translation Finir (to finish) Translation Vendre (to sell) Translation
Je J'ai parlé I spoke / I have spoken J'ai fini I finished / I have finished J'ai vendu I sold / I have sold
Tu Tu as parlé You spoke / You have spoken Tu as fini You finished / You have finished Tu as vendu You sold / You have sold
Il/Elle/On Il/Elle a parlé He/She/One spoke / has spoken Il/Elle a fini He/She/One finished / has finished Il/Elle a vendu He/She/One sold / has sold
Nous Nous avons parlé We spoke / We have spoken Nous avons fini We finished / We have finished Nous avons vendu We sold / We have sold
Vous Vous avez parlé You spoke / You have spoken Vous avez fini You finished / You have finished Vous avez vendu You sold / You have sold
Ils/Elles Ils/Elles ont parlé They spoke / They have spoken Ils/Elles ont fini They finished / They have finished Ils/Elles ont vendu They sold / They have sold

2. Passé Composé with "Être" (Dr. & Mrs. Vandertramp Verbs)

Some verbs use être instead of **avoir** as the auxiliary. These are mostly movement verbs.

Subject Aller (to go) Translation
Je Je suis allé(e) I went / I have gone
Tu Tu es allé(e) You went / You have gone
Il/Elle/On Il est allé / Elle est allée He/She/One went / has gone
Nous Nous sommes allé(e)s We went / We have gone
Vous Vous êtes allé(e)(s) You went / You have gone
Ils/Elles Ils sont allés / Elles sont allées They went / They have gone

Common Irregular Verbs

Some verbs have irregular past participles:

  • Avoir → eu (J'ai eu – I had)
  • Être → été (J'ai été – I was)
  • Faire → fait (J'ai fait – I did/made)
  • Prendre → pris (J'ai pris – I took)
  • Voir → vu (J'ai vu – I saw)
  • Mettre → mis (J'ai mis – I put)
  • Dire → dit (J'ai dit – I said)
  • Venir → venu (Je suis venu – I came)

The Passé Composé is commonly used in spoken French to describe past events.
